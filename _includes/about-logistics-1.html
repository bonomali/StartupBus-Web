YOUR MISSION

Jump on a bus, conceive, build and launch a startup with a team of strangers in 72 hours at 70 mph – then pitch Techstars on stage at Boulder Startup Week.

DESTINATION: BOULDER STARTUP WEEK

On May 15th, the best and the brightest will come together to compete in StartupBus Americas VII, the continent’s most epic competition – combing the magic of a hackathon and a road trip to create the ultimate adventure on wheels.


We’ll be hitting all the raddest hot spots and tech shops as we make our way to Boulder Startup Week, where buses from 3 countries will converge in the quest for StartupBus glory. This year each bus is going to have a theme, so you can choose your own adventure. Finalists will get the chance to pitch on stage for Techstars at the historic Boulder Theater.
